ABOUT WINE PALACE

Wine Palace Holdings Ltd is a fast-growing wine and spirits merchant. We came in to existence with the vision of providing clients with the best wines sourced directly from top châteaux and domaines - alongside specialist consultancy from world-class trade professionals. We work closely with hotels and big corporations, as well as directly with private clients. Currently we have three retail shops located in Shenzhen premium commercial area and a flagship store in Hong Kong.
